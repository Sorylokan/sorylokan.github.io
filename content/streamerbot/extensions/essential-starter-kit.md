### **🔄️ Automations:**
* **Discord Embed**
  - _📣 Automatically announces your stream on Discord. (Provided by [\[WEB•UI\]](https://discord.com/channels/834650675224248362/1465537047547740283/1465537047547740283))_

  - _💬 Regular messages to share info in chat._
* **Raid Shoutout**
  - _🤩 Automatically thanks streamers who raid you._

  - _👋 Welcomes your viewers as soon as they enter chat._

  - _✨ Sends messages to thank new followers as they join._

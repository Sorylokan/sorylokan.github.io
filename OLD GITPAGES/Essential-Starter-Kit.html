Essential Starter Kit

Essential Starter Kit is a turnkey solution designed for beginners or streamers who want quick, functional setups with StreamerBot. This kit simplifies setting up streams with essential controls and interactive automations.

"Perfect for beginners, streamers in a hurry, or professional procrastinators!"

Key Features

  • Discord Embed: Automatically announces streams on Discord.
  • Chat Announcements: Share regular info messages in chat.
  • Raid Shoutout: Thanks streamers for raids automatically.
  • Greets: Welcomes viewers when they enter chat.
  • Follower Thanks: Sends messages to thank new followers.
  • Sub & Gift Sub Thanks: Thank subscribers and gift-givers.

Tutorial

Setting up the Essential Starter Kit is straightforward:

  1. Download the Essential_Starter_Kit.sb file.
  2. Open Streamer.bot and click the "Import" button.
  3. Drag the file into the "Import String" field and validate.
  4. Edit details like social links and Discord settings.
